# Model Comparison Day 64 Week 6 Exploration Summary

Week 6 begins with a frozen decision policy:

- lowest MAE identifies the measurement leader;
- a learned candidate must improve baseline MAE by at least 10%;
- learned candidates within 0.25 MAE units are practically equivalent;
- lower complexity breaks practical ties;
- RMSE, contextual R² and largest errors remain visible diagnostics;
- one deterministic holdout does not provide stability evidence.

The eventual status is limited to `selected_for_next_integration`. No
production-readiness, forecasting or inventory-action claim is permitted.

Day 64 adds planning and validation only. Formal tables, errors, selection and
model cards belong to Days 65–68.
