# Day 122 — Coverage and Risk Summary

- Coverage is measured in days from observed demand and current stock.
- Bread has 0.0 coverage days and priority score 100.0.
- Milk has 1.5 coverage days and priority score 50.0.
- Sufficient-stock products currently score 0.0.
- A triggered product receives a minimum watch score of 25 at the exact boundary.
- Zero observed demand has no finite coverage and score 0.0.
- Scores are deterministic priority indices, never probabilities.
