# Sprint 1 — Week 4 Exploration Summary

## Status

Day 22 completed. Week 4 is open and Sprint 1 remains open.

## Selected product flow

```text
React dashboard
→ GET /api/v1/demand-insights/summary
→ thin FastAPI route
→ internal Demand Summary service
→ validated Sprint 1 artifacts
```

The dependency direction is deliberate: presentation depends on a versioned API,
the route delegates to an internal service, and the service consumes evidence
already generated by the analytical production modules.

## Contract decision

The response uses schema version `1.0` and groups period, sales summary, baseline,
leaders, five Insight Cards and limitations. Missing or invalid required evidence
produces `503 Service Unavailable`; no layer fabricates business values.

## Day boundaries

- Day 22 defines the flow and contract only.
- Day 23 implements the internal service.
- Day 24 implements the endpoint and HTTP schemas.
- Day 25 implements the initial dashboard and visible cards.
- Day 26 integrates the existing figures.
