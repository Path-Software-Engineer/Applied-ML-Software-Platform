# v1.0.0 — Retail Intelligence Platform

Release: `v1.0.0-retail-intelligence-platform`  
Status: **release-ready — Gitflow mechanics authorized**

## Product capability

This release completes one staged decision-support product: Demand Insight
describes observed sales, Model Comparison evaluates learning candidates, and
Inventory Decision turns current evidence into traceable human-review priorities.

## Included

- three versioned schema 1.0 read resources in one FastAPI service;
- three isolated React features in one accessible staged shell;
- reproducible analytical, model and inventory artifacts;
- Insight Cards, Decision Cards and Recommendation Cards;
- product-level inventory trace and three official inventory figures;
- unified evidence generation, complete quality gate and local runbook.

## Verified evidence

- Demand Insight: 293 units and 747.65 revenue;
- Model Comparison: Gradient Boosting observed MAE leader; Random Forest selected
  for next integration under practical equivalence;
- Inventory Decision: six products, two review priorities and 97 suggested
  review units;
- 181 Python tests, 31 frontend tests and 85 manual checks passed at the final
  release gate on Day 147.

## Known limitations

- small synthetic data and no external validation;
- model evidence is not production ready;
- observed demand is descriptive rather than forecast;
- stock and lead-time evidence are not live supplier data;
- no purchase orders, authentication or production deployment;
- responsive browser screenshots depend on local browser policy and are not
  replaced by mockups.
